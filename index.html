Kerala

God's Own Country!

Kerala, fondly referred to as ‘God’s Own Country’, is located on the south-western tip of India and enjoys unique geographical features that have made it a paradise on Earth. The western side of this land is lined by nearly 600KM of Arabian Sea shoreline and the inlands are filled with lush-green forests and mountain slopes of Western-ghats.

Kerala was selected by the National Geographic Traveller as one of the 50 destinations of a lifetime and one of the thirteen paradises in the world.

Prime Destinations


Thiruvananthapuram

Capital city of Kerala, distinguished by its British colonial architecture and many art galleries.
Thiruvananthapuram has historically been a cultural hub in South India due to the development of arts, architecture and liberal customs by the rulers of erstwhile Trivandrum.

Alappuzha

Widely known as Venice of the East, houseboat cruises in Alappuzha along the rustic Kerala backwaters, a network of tranquil canals and lagoons is an exquisite experience. Punnamada Lake's snake boat races is also well known and held once every year.

Idukki

Densely forested, mountainous region, home to the Anamudi mountain where the rare, blue Neelakurinji flower blooms every 12 years.Nearby, Munnar is a hill station known for its sprawling tea plantations and Tea Museum. Farther south is the vast, curved Idukki Dam and Periyar National Park.

Wayanad

Wayanad, the green paradise is nestled among the mountains of the Western Ghats, forming the border world of the greener part of Kerala. Clean and pristine, enchanting and hypnotizing, this land is filled with history and culture.





Why To Visit Kerala?
  • Sandwiched between the Western Ghats mountains and the Arabian Sea, dotted with magical backwaters, lush greenery, wildlife, pristine beaches, spice and tea plantations and tantalizing cuisines, Kerala has the perfect recipe to be your next perfect vacation.
  • Ayurveda is a perfect ancient science of life; and Kerala is the home of Ayurveda, one of the oldest forms of traditional medicine in the world, which focuses on a holistic approach to wellbeing. Important Ayurvedic techniques include yoga, meditation, diet, massage and the application of oils. Kerala is a pioneer of Ayurvedic treatments and many hotels and resorts offer treatments in luxurious, peaceful settings.
  • Kerala is a heaven for the lover of dance, drama and music. The art & culture of Kerala always attracts travelers from all parts of the country. You have scores of art forms, which are quite unique in nature to Kerala, Watch them and experience an endless and varied emotions to win your passion, love, kindness, compassion, mirth, joy, sorrow, grief, anger, wonder, horror, fear, valour, courage etc.
